Geographic Exposure

Analyze your portfolio's geographic diversification and currency exposure. Canadian investors often exhibit "home bias" — overweighting domestic securities despite Canada representing only ~3% of global market capitalization. A well-diversified portfolio typically includes exposure to US, international developed, and emerging markets.

Portfolio Risk Models

Quantitative risk analysis using industry-standard models. Value at Risk (VaR) estimates maximum potential loss at confidence levels, Monte Carlo simulation generates thousands of scenarios for outcome distribution, and multi-factor analysis decomposes risk across market, size, value, and momentum factors. Calculations use estimated volatility based on asset class characteristics.
